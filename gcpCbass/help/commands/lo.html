lo bands, stages, state, [ant]

Switch one or more local oscillator stages on or off.

Arguments:
LoBands bands
The band whose oscillator is to be controlled.
LoStages stages
The oscillator stages to be enabled or disabled.
SwitchState state
The desired state of the selected stages: on or off.

Example:
The following example turns on the sweep stage of the Xband oscillator 
 lo xband, sweep, on
