The cabinShutter command (usage)

Context:

The cabinShutter command is used to open or close the cabin shutter, which, when closed, prevents melting of the foam window on the secondary optics cryostat if the telescope is pointed at the Sun, and also is inteded to drive any latched bolometers normal by exposing them to radiation from an object at ambient temperature.

Examples:

The following example opens the cabin shutter 
  cabinShutter open
The following example closes the cabin shutter 
  cabinShutter close
