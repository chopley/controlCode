The benchFocus command (usage)

Arguments:

BenchFocusOffset focus
Offset of bench position along focus axis (more positive is away from primary).

Context:

The benchFocus command is intended to be used in focus scripts to move the optical bench toward and away from the primary along the axis of the primary beam.

Examples:

The following example moves the optical bench 5 millimeters farther from the primary along the beam axis. 
  benchFocus 5.0
