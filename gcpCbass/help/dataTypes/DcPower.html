DcPower

A downconverter power level

Input Format:

An Double a power level, or the word 'preset', to iterate to a preset power level, where applicable.

Examples:
The following example sets the noise source power level to a preset power level: 
  noisePower preset
The following example sets the quad mod power level to 10.4: 
  quadPower 10.4
