The PagerState data type (synopsis)

A choice datatype.

Choices are:
on
Activate the pager
off
Deactivate the pager (for sustained paging devices
chip
email
disable
Disable the pager altogether
enable
Enable the pager
clear
list
status
Get a status report from the modem pager
