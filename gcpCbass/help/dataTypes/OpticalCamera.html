The OpticalCamera datatype.
OpticalCamera

The target of the optical_camera command.

Input Format:

An option from:
  • camera - The optical camera.
  • stepper - The optical camera stepper motor.
  • focus - The camera focus.

Context:
In addition to its radio receivers, SPT has three small optical telescopes. This is used for determining the major parts of the pointing model. The OpticalCamera datatype allows one to select an optical camera function to control. If camera or stepper is chosen, the next argument to the optical_camera command should be the word on or off, specifying the desired state of the selected device. If focus, the next argument should be the integral number of steps to step the stepper motor.
