Specifying page colors.

Valid colors are simple color names like blue and red, or numerically specified colors like #rrggbb, where rr is the amount of red in the color, specified as a hexadecimal number, gg is the amount of green and bb is the amount of blue. To see the full list of recognized color names, type: 
 showrgb
in a terminal window.
Erik Leitch (4-Nov-1999)
