Deleting an unwanted graph

To delete an unwanted graph, put the cursor into deletion mode by either hitting key "d" within the plot window, or by pulling down the Cursor menu of the plot window. The cursor will be displayed as a cross hair at this point. After moving the center of the cross hair cursor over the unwanted graph, click the right mouse button. The graph will then disappear, and the remaining graphs will expand to take up the vacated space. Be warned that until a new input mode is selected from the Cursor menu, the cursor remains in deletion mode after deleting a graph, so clicking a second time would delete another graph.
Erik Leitch (4-Nov-1999)
