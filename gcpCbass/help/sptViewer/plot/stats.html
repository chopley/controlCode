Requesting the statistics of a given displayed trace

To find out the statistics of the displayed portion of a given register trace, start by switching to the statistics cursor-input mode, either by pressing the "s" key within the plot window, or by pulling down the Cursor menu of the plot window and selecting Stats. The cursor will change to cross-hairs, and thereafter, until the cursor input mode is changed, pressing the left mouse button anywhere within the bounds of the plot will result in the statistics of the trace associated with the nearest point to the cursor being reported in a text area just below the plot.
Erik Leitch (6-Nov-1999)
